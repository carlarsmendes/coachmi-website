How do we do this?

Coachmi provides ICF certified, on-site and remote, professional coaching sessions for both individuals and groups.

Particularly focused on the development of young adults, Coachmi also works as a supportive community by creating space for sharing ideas and experiences around self development.

Choose what suits you better and begin the change

Face-to-face coaching session

Individual

You will meet your coach personally everytime you book a session. The place and day where to meet is previously agreed according to your availability. We also have the possibility of alternative locations such as sailing boats, walking coaching, and others, which can vary according to your interest and goal.

Remote coaching session

Individual

You will e-meet your coach through a skype call. For this option you will need a computer or phone anywhere around the world. This option is available for anyone living outside of Lisbon. It is also an affordable option to save time and allows more flexible scheduling.

Group coaching

Group

Meet a group of like-minded people who are undergoing a similar process of change. During this group coaching process, each session has a concrete goal agreed by all coachees. It is a more affordable option which allows you to grow along with your peers by sharing challenges and experiences while working to achieve your individual goal.
